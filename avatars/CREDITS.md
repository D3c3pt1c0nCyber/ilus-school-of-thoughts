# Debater portraits — credits

Portrait avatars used in the Debate Arena bot queue. All subjects died well over
70 years ago and the source images are public-domain busts, paintings, engravings,
or pre-1929 photographs obtained from **Wikimedia Commons** via the Wikipedia REST API.
Each image was centre-cropped to a square and downscaled (256×256) for use as an avatar.
