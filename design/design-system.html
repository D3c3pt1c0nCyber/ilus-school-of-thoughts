03 — Values
Design Principles
The school's four governing values, embedded in every design decision.
I.
Scholarship before spectacle
Typography and legibility come first. Decoration earns its place only if it communicates — the parchment texture, the seal, the brass rule.
II.
Warmth, not coldness
Parchment over white. Spectral over sans-serif. The school should feel like a well-lit library, not a SaaS dashboard.
III.
Level signals are always explicit
Verd / Gold / Garnet — the three level colors appear consistently on every surface where level matters. Never swap them.
IV.
Charitable to the student
Progress is never hidden. Locks are explained, never punitive. Toast feedback is always kind. The UI should never make a learner feel judged.
