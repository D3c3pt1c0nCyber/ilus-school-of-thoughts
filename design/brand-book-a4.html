03 — Design Principles
  • I. Scholarship before spectacleTypography and legibility first. Decoration earns its place only if it communicates.
  • II. Warmth, not coldnessParchment over white. Spectral over sans. The school is a library, not a dashboard.
  • III. Level signals are always explicitVerd / Gold / Garnet — consistent on every surface. Never swap.
  • IV. Charitable to the studentProgress is never hidden. Locks are explained. Feedback is always kind.
